What we offer

A Local, Personal
Daycare Alternative

We are a small, neighbourhood-based alternative to large daycare centres. Low numbers, familiar faces, and genuine one-to-one attention in a setting your dog actually enjoys.

01
Daily Exercise

Multiple walks throughout the day in your local parks and streets. Small group sizes mean every dog gets real attention, not just a crowd to get lost in.

02
Doorstep Collection

We come to you. Our local collection service picks your dog up from your front door and drops them back, so your day stays uninterrupted.

03
Flexible Packages

Full-day, half-day and walk-only options, built around your week. No rigid contracts or minimum commitments, just care that fits your life.

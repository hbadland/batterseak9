What we offer

A Local, Personal
Daycare Alternative

We are a small, neighbourhood-based alternative to large daycare centres. Low numbers, familiar faces, and genuine one-to-one attention in a setting your dog actually enjoys.

01
Daily Exercise

Multiple walks throughout the day in your local parks and streets. Small group sizes mean every dog gets real attention, not just a crowd to get lost in.

02
Doorstep Collection

We come to you. Our local collection service picks your dog up from your front door and drops them back, so your day stays uninterrupted.

03
Flexible Packages

Full-day, half-day and walk-only options, built around your week. No rigid contracts or minimum commitments, just care that fits your life.

Expert training

Professional
Training Programs

Led by Jack, a qualified trainer with over 10 years experience across military and civilian settings.

  • Puppy Foundation ClassesEssential socialisation and basic obedience for puppies aged 8 to 16 weeks, setting them up for lifelong success.
  • Basic Obedience TrainingMaster essential commands including sit, stay, come, down, and loose-leash walking in various environments.
  • Advanced ObedienceBuild on basic skills with off-leash reliability, distance commands, and complex behaviour sequences.
  • Behaviour ModificationAddress challenges like reactivity, anxiety, jumping, or excessive barking with customised training plans.
  • Group ClassesLearn alongside other dogs in a supportive environment that builds confidence and real-world skills.

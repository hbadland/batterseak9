Professional Training Programs

Training programs led by Jack, a qualified dog trainer with over 10 years experience in military and civillian dog training.

  • Puppy Foundation Classes Essential socialization and basic obedience for puppies aged 8-16 weeks, setting them up for lifelong success.
  • Basic Obedience Training Master essential commands including sit, stay, come, down, and loose-leash walking in various environments.
  • Advanced Obedience Build on basic skills with off-leash reliability, distance commands, and complex behavior sequences.
  • Behavior Modification Address specific challenges like reactivity, anxiety, jumping, or excessive barking with customized training plans.
  • Group Classes Learn alongside other dogs in a supportive environment that builds confidence and real-world skills.
